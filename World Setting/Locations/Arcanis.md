# Arcanis

- **Type:** Capital City of Eldoria
- **Description:** A massive city of white stone and magic.
- **Key Locations:**
  - **Royal Palace:** Center of the city, floating bridges.
  - **City Watch Headquarters:** Dungeon located in the basement.
  - **Market District:** Chaotic, colorful stalls.
  - **The Severed Cock:** Dive bar in the slums.
  - **Plaza of the First King:** Main square with a fountain.
- **State:**
  - **Chapter 1-2:** Tense, rumors of war.
  - **Chapter 3:** Under martial law, site of the "First Spark" incident. War declared.
