# World State

**Current Era:** The Age of Shadows (Beginning)
**Date:** 3rd Era, Year 503, Late Spring.

## Major Events
- **The First Spark:** War between Eldoria and Aridia.
- **The Domino Effect:** Demon Horde incursion.
- **The Awakening:** Magical disturbances. Wildlife mutating.
- **The Call:** Grand Alliance Summit at Neutral Spire.
- **The Alliance Forms:** Alliance of Light established.
- **The Demon Generals:** Alliance Base destroyed.
- **The Mightiest Hero:** Aric dead.
- **The Fall:** Alliance Army routed.
- **The Rout:** Ancient Vault discovered.
- **The Siege:** "Light of the Stars" activated.
- **The Holy Dome:** Dome active.
- **The Unforgiving Light:** Book 1 Concluded. The Exiled Resistance formed in the Outer Lands.

## Political Status
- **Alliance of Light:** Split.
- **Inner Kingdoms (Eldoria, Sylvanaria, Drakoria, Aetheria):** Safe behind Dome. Rebuilding.
- **Outer Lands (Aridia, Kharaz'Mar):** Demon Controlled. Guerilla war by The Exiled.
- **The Exiled:** New faction led by Kaelen/Elfiria/Grok.

## Locations
- **Valley of Kings:** Ground Zero.
- **Outer Lands:** Base of operations for The Exiled.

- **Shadow Energy:** Dominant outside.
