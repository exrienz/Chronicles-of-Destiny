# The Vane Letter

- **Type:** Quest Item / Plot Device
- **Origin:** Lord Valerius of House Vane.
- **Content:** Proof that the border attacks are "False Flags" orchestrated by Shadow Agents to incite war between Eldoria and Aridia. Mentions "Feeding Him" (The Demon King).
- **Current Holder:** Lyra.
- **Status:** The information is technically "useless" as of Chapter 3, since war was declared before it could be presented. However, it remains proof of the conspiracy.
