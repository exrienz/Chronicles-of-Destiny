# Locations

## Eldoria

### Oakhaven
- **Type:** Border Town
- **Description:** A small, dusty settlement near the Whispering Woods. It serves as a stopover for travelers and merchants entering Eldoria from the south. The architecture is simple, mostly wood and thatch.
- **Key Locations:**
  - **The Rusty Blade:** A run-down tavern frequented by locals and low-level mercenaries.

### Arcanis
- **Type:** Capital City
- **Description:** The gleaming capital of Eldoria, known as the City of Light. It is built in concentric circles, with the royal palace at the center. The city is protected by ancient wards.
- **Key Locations:**
  - **The Severed Cock:** A seedy tavern in the outer slums where illicit deals often happen.
  - **The Royal Palace:** The seat of power, currently rife with political tension.

## The Whispering Woods
- **Type:** Forest
- **Description:** An ancient forest bordering Oakhaven. The trees are massive and the canopy is thick, creating perpetual twilight. It is rumored to be haunted, but mostly it is just a haven for bandits and wild beasts.

## Unexplored Lands

### Spire of Anguish
- **Type:** Demonic Stronghold
- **Description:** A jagged peak where the veil between the material plane and the Void is thin. It serves as a base of operations for the Demon Generals.
