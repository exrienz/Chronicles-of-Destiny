# Character Compendium

## Core Cast
- **Kael Ardor** (Human, Eldoria) – Battle-mage who weaves sigils with swordplay. Pragmatic, secretly sentimental; fears losing control of his destructive magic. Uses dry humor as a shield.
- **Lyria Skydance** (Aetherian) – Aerial duelist and engineer of whimwheels. Competitive, flirtatious, loves puns. Keeps a charm that records whispered hopes.
- **Rowan Thorne** (Beastkin from Sylvanaria) – Scout with heightened senses and a knack for pranks. Values loyalty; teases to test trust. Romantic slow-burn with Kael.
- **Seris Valen** (Kharaz'Mar Runist) – Scholar-warrior who designs runic interfaces. Stoic exterior hides intense passion for justice. Curious about blending magitech and intimacy rituals.
- **Elfiria Lumen** (Leader of Aetheria) – Wields the Holy Dome relic. Diplomatic, visionary; carries grief for those lost in war. Repressed desire for connection due to duty.

## Antagonists and Foils
- **Demon King Nihraz** – Feeds on despair; charming and cruel. Seeks the Night Bloom to invert the Holy Dome.
- **General Myrka** – Demon general who manipulates politics through glamor illusions; revels in awkward humor.
- **The Veilborne** – Shadow cabal operatives; key agent known as **Whisper** trades secrets for affection.

## Relationship Tracker
- **Kael ↔ Lyria**: Playful rivalry turning romantic; share glide-rail races and trinket exchanges. Tension spikes during co-engineering tasks.
- **Kael ↔ Rowan**: Deepening trust from battlefield rescues; humor and scent-based teasing lead to intimate mana-sharing trial.
- **Lyria ↔ Seris**: Intellectual chemistry over runic designs; occasional banter competitions.
- **Seris ↔ Elfiria**: Mutual respect; potential tenderness when discussing burden of leadership.

## Comedic Traits
- Kael's spells occasionally misfire to produce harmless glitter storms.
- Lyria bets embarrassing favors on every race.
- Rowan leaves illusionary tail ribbons on friends' armor.
- Seris tells deadpan jokes that land minutes later.

## Intimacy Notes
- Mana-sharing requires synchronized breathing; trust scenes emphasize emotional consent.
- Private vows use scented candles keyed to personal mana scents (spice for Kael, sky-breeze for Lyria, pine for Rowan, ember-metal for Seris).
