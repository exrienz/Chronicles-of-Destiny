# Character Profiles

## Main Characters

### Kaelen
- **Role:** Protagonist
- **Race:** Human
- **Job:** Swordsman (Aiming for Blademaster / Hidden: Sword Saint)
- **Affiliation:** Independent / Alliance of Light (later)
- **Appearance:** Messy black hair, sharp blue eyes, lean but muscular build. Wears light armor to prioritize speed.
- **Personality:** Determined, slightly cynical but deeply protective. Has a dry wit and tends to make sarcastic comments in dangerous situations. Struggling with a past failure that makes him hesitant to lead.
- **Background:** A survivor of a destroyed border village. He has been wandering as a mercenary, honing his dual-wielding skills. He possesses a latent potential that reacts to high-stress combat.
- **Dynamics:**
  - **Elfiria:** Initially sees her as an unreachable royal, but their relationship develops into a deep emotional and romantic bond as he becomes her protector.
  - **Lyra:** Constant bickering partner; they share a "siblings-in-arms" dynamic that teases at romance.

### Elfiria
- **Role:** Main Heroine / Political Leader
- **Race:** Aasimar
- **Job:** High Priestess (Hidden: Seraphic Herald)
- **Affiliation:** Kingdom of Aetheria (Queen/Leader)
- **Appearance:** Radiantly beautiful, long silver hair, golden eyes, glowing skin. Dressed in ceremonial white and gold robes that can shift for combat.
- **Personality:** Graceful, compassionate, but burdened by the weight of her crown. She has a hidden playful side that only surfaces around those she trusts. Surprisingly bold in romantic situations when she drops her "queen" mask.
- **Background:** The young ruler of Aetheria. She holds the "Holy Dome" artifact. She feels the pressure of being the "symbol of hope" and yearns for a connection where she can just be a woman, not a queen.

### Lyra
- **Role:** Main Support / Love Interest
- **Race:** Elf
- **Job:** Sniper (Archer path)
- **Affiliation:** Sylvanaria / Kaelen's Party
- **Appearance:** Tall, athletic, green hair braided back, emerald eyes, pointed ears. Wears practical leather armor.
- **Personality:** Teasing, confident, and flirtatious. She enjoys flustering Kaelen. Beneath the humor, she is deadly serious about protecting her friends.
- **Background:** An exile from Sylvanaria due to her refusal to adhere to traditional isolationism. She seeks to prove that Elves should engage with the world.

### Drakon
- **Role:** Rival / Best Friend
- **Race:** Humanoid Dragon (Drakoria)
- **Job:** Dragon Knight (Custom mix of Warrior and Fire Magic)
- **Affiliation:** Drakoria
- **Appearance:** Hulking figure, patches of red scales on skin, horns, tail.
- **Personality:** Boisterous, loves battle and food. Takes everything literally, leading to comedic misunderstandings. fiercely loyal.
- **Background:** A prince of Drakoria sent to learn about the "soft skins" (humans). He respects strength above all else.

## Supporting Characters

### Aric (The Fallen Hero)
- **Role:** Mentor / Tragic Hero
- **Race:** Human
- **Job:** Sword Saint (Legendary)
- **Affiliation:** Alliance of Light (Commander)
- **Status:** Falls in battle against the Demon King, setting the stakes.
- **Personality:** Stoic, inspiring, invincible-seeming.

### General Malakor
- **Role:** Antagonist (One of the 7 Demon Generals)
- **Race:** Demon
- **Job:** Dark Knight
- **Appearance:** Ebon armor, burning red eyes.
- **Personality:** Cruel, calculating, enjoys breaking hope.
