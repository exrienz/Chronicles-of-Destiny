# Plot and Continuity Tracker

## Global Threads
- Search for the **Night Bloom** relic as counterbalance to Holy Dome.
- Veilborne infiltration of Alliance politics; Whisper targets council aides.
- Demonic advance from Noxreach creating refugee crises in Outlands towns.

## Book 1 Focus
- Establish core team within Alliance forces stationed near Eldoria's border fort **Sunspoke Keep**.
- Uncover first Veilborne plot to sabotage glide-rails and frame Sylvanaria scouts.
- Introduce Night Bloom legend via ancient tablet recovered in Chapter 4.
- Early romantic sparks: Kael/Lyria rivalry; Kael/Rowan shared survival; Seris/Elfiria duty-bound longing.
- Comic motif: prank illusions interfering with formal ceremonies.

- **Chapter 1**: Kael and Lyria trade barbs during glide-rail race; Rowan sneaks in prank ribbons.

- **Chapter 3**: Council debate humor; first intimate mana-sharing between Kael and Lyria to power shield.
- **Chapter 4**: Rowan comforts Kael after nightmare; tension shifts to softer humor.
- **Chapter 5**: Seris and Elfiria discuss burden of command; subtle hand-touch.

## Location Notes
- **Sunspoke Keep**: Alliance border fortress with glide-rail hub, tavern "Glitter Mug", and training yards.
- **Aetherian Skybridge**: Floating platform used for diplomatic arrivals.
- **Outlands Village Ashway**: First demonic attack site; reveals Veilborne sabotage.
