# Drakon

- **Role:** Rival / Best Friend
- **Race:** Humanoid Dragon (Drakoria)
- **Job:** Dragon Knight (Custom mix of Warrior and Fire Magic)
- **Affiliation:** Drakoria
- **Level:** 6 (Chapter 1) -> 7 (Chapter 3)
- **Appearance:** Hulking figure (7ft+), patches of red scales on skin, horns, tail. Wears heavy plate mixed with leather.
- **Personality:** Boisterous, loves battle and food. Takes everything literally, leading to comedic misunderstandings. Fiercely loyal. Has "Diplomatic Immunity".
- **Combat Style:** Brute force. Hand-to-hand or heavy weapons. Can breathe fire (limited). High resistance to fire/heat.
- **Inventory:**
  - Massive Fists
  - Scavenged Armor
  - Snacks
