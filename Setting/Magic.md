# Magic & Lore

## Magic Systems

### Aether / Celestial Magic
- **Source:** Divine connection or celestial lineage (Aasimar).
- **Users:** Priests, Paladins, Aasimar.
- **Effects:** Healing, protection, light-based attacks, banishing darkness.
- **Key Artifacts:** The Holy Dome (Aetheria).

### Elemental Magic
- **Source:** The natural world (Fire, Water, Earth, Air).
- **Users:** Mages, Sorcerers, Elemental races (Dragons, Merfolk, Fire Elementals).
- **Effects:** Manipulation of elements for combat or utility.

### Shadow / Void Magic
- **Source:** The Void, an extra-dimensional plane of darkness and entropy.
- **Users:** Demon Generals, Shadow Agents, Necromancers.
- **Effects:** Stealth, corruption, mind manipulation, decaying life force.
- **Lore:** Void magic is inherently corrupting. It feeds on negative emotions (fear, grief, sorrow).

## Major Factions

### The Alliance of Light
- A coalition of 5 kingdoms (Eldoria, Sylvanaria, Drakoria, Kharaz'Mar, Aetheria) formed to oppose the Demon King.

### The Shadow Forces
- Agents corrupted by or serving the Demon King. They infiltrate governments to sow discord.
- **Elite Assassins:** Modified warriors with enhanced speed and strength, often using poisoned weapons.

### The Demon Army
- **The Demon King:** An ancient entity of pure void energy.
- **The Seven Generals:** Powerful commanders, each representing a facet of suffering (e.g., Malakor, Xylos).
